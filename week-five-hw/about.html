About Us

These are the special people who make this special place escpecially special.

Tom Haverford

Founder, Entrepreneur, Business mogul

"Thomas Montgomery Haverford is an Indian American,[1] who changed his name from Darwish Sabir Ismael Gani to be more appealing in politics.[2] He is often assumed to be an immigrant by much of the Parks and Recreation staff (Leslie often assuming him to be Libyan), though, as he frequently reminds them, he hails from South Carolina.[3] Tom displays an extremely sarcastic, mischievous and cocky attitude,[3][4] and frequently attempts to secure favors from local contractors. Tom has demonstrated his talent and passion for entrepreneurship in many occasions, including Entertainment 720, Rent-A-Swag, and Tom's Bistro." Wikipedia

Craig Middlebrooks
